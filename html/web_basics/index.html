Quick Tips to Make the Best Pie Eva

  1. The most important thing you can do is make sure your water and butter are very cold
  2. DO NOT knead dough:
    • Cut through butter with a knife, your hands, or a food processor
  3. Use a silicone mat or clear wrap paper to roll dough out, this ensures easier transfer to pie plate
  4. When making fruit pies, make sure the fruit is ripe and sweet before using it
